Al-Doha Academy

Al-Doha Academy is a specialized educational platform in teaching the Holy Quran and its sciences, providing distinguished education according to the highest educational standards with qualified teaching staff.

We aim to deliver correct Islamic knowledge through innovative methods suitable for all age groups, while maintaining authenticity and quality in education.

Our Vision

To be pioneers in teaching the Holy Quran and its sciences remotely, and to reach our distinguished educational methodology to every student anywhere in the world.

  • Excellence in teaching the Holy Quran and its sciences
  • Leadership in Islamic e-learning
  • Expansion to meet the needs of Muslims everywhere

Our Mission

To spread the sciences of the Holy Quran and teach the Arabic language with a correct scientific methodology, while preserving the continuous chain of transmission, using the latest educational technologies.

We strive to provide distinguished education that combines authenticity and modernity, with a focus on quality and efficiency in delivering information.

Our Goals

Teaching the Holy Quran

Spreading the knowledge of the Quranic sciences and assisting the greatest number of people in its memorization, proper recitation (Tajweed), and understand the Book of Allah.

Contributing to the development of a generation that is both keen on and masters the Holy Quran.

The academy focuses on sound upbringing alongside education and training.

Teaching Arabic Language

Teaching Arabic language to non-native speakers

Interactive Learning Environment

Providing an interactive and stimulating learning environment

Our Educational Programs

Quran Kareem

An integrated program for teaching Quran recitation and memorization, taking into account Tajweed rules, suitable for all ages and levels.

  • Teaching correct recitation
  • Memorizing the entire Quran or parts of it
  • Reviewing memorized portions
  • Practical Tajweed application

Ijazahs with Sanad

Granting certifications in the Ten Minor and Major Qira'at with a continuous chain of transmission to the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), according to approved certification standards.

  • Certification in Hafs from Asim
  • Certification in the Ten Minor Qira'at
  • Certification in the Ten Major Qira'at
  • Certification in Warsh or Qalun narration
